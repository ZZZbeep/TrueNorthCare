Founded to Make Sure No Veteran's Medical Evidence Holds Their Claim Back

Your Questions, Answered
A nexus letter is a formal medical opinion written by a clinician that establishes the link between your current medical condition and your military service. It is one of the most critical pieces of evidence in a VA disability claim.
Not always, but in many cases a nexus letter significantly strengthens your claim — especially if your condition wasn't diagnosed during service or if the VA has previously denied your claim.
Direct service connection means your condition was caused by military service. Secondary service connection means a condition was caused or aggravated by an already service-connected disability.
